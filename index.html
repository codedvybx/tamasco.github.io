TAMASCO


Tamale Senior High School, located in Tamale, Ghana, stands as a beacon of education and community development in the Northern Region. Established in 1951 with a commitment to providing quality education.
Tamasco aims be a provider of quality education to its students so that they can acquire the requisite qualification for further training in tertiary institutions and become responsible and productive citizens ready to assume leadership roles in the country.
